# Google Play Store Metadata

## App Title
PayBills - Utility Payments Simplified

## Short Description
The easiest way to pay utility bills, buy data/airtime, and shop online.

## Long Description
PayBills is designed to simplify your daily financial interactions. No more complex fintech jargon—just fast, reliable utility payments and global shopping power.

From recharging your phone to paying for power and cable TV, PayBills handles it all in seconds. Our premium virtual cards also allow you to shop on international sites without hassle.

**Key Features:**
- **Seamless Utilities**: Pay for Electricity, Water, and Cable TV instantly.
- **Fast Recharge**: Airtime and Data for MTN, Airtel, Glo, and 9mobile.
- **Global Virtual Cards**: Instant USD/NGN cards for all your online shopping.
- **Simplified Ledger**: Track every kobo with our clean and intuitive dashboard.
- **Bank-Grade Security**: Your data and funds are protected by multi-layer encryption.
